My Certificates & Qualifications

I’ve completed certified training in English communication, Database Management (Department of Youth Development),
216-hour Graphics Design (Bangladesh Hi-Tech Park), and 60-hour Video Production & Editing
(CUET & BCC EDGE Project). These programs strengthened my skills across language, design, data handling, and digital media.

1.Google

Awarded by Google, this certificate recognizes the successful completion of the Google Ads Display Certification. Valid from August 11, 2025, to August 11, 2026, this achievement highlights my expertise in creating impactful display advertising strategies to drive measurable business results.

2. Video Production & Editing Training:

Completed a specialized 60-hour training on Video Production and Editing, organized by the Department of CSE, Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology, under the EDGE project of the Bangladesh Computer Council. The program focused on storytelling, cinematography, audio-video syncing, and editing software, empowering participants with the skills to produce professional-grade video content.

3. Graphics Design Certification:

Successfully completed an intensive 216-hour professional training program in Graphics Design, conducted by Logical Triangle Ltd., NBR Jobs Ltd. & ZayanZara Ltd. – JV, and supported by Bangladesh Hi-Tech Park Authority. The course emphasized hands-on experience with industry-standard design tools and techniques, enabling the development of creative visual solutions for both print and digital media.

4.Database Management Course:

Earned a government-recognized certification in Database Management, offered by the Department of Youth Development, Ministry of Youth & Sports. This course provided foundational to intermediate-level training on data storage, retrieval, and management using relational database systems, preparing candidates for effective handling of data in real-world applications.

English

Successfully completed the British Council Foundation English Test on March 4, 2024, organized under the Sheikh Kamal IT Training and Incubation Program. The test, aligned with the CEFR framework, assessed key language skills — Listening, Reading, Speaking, and Writing. Md. Nurul Alam achieved an overall B1 grade, scoring 65 out of 80, with strong performances in Speaking (18/20), Writing (17/20), and Listening (16/20) at B1 level, and Reading (14/20) at A2 level. This certification signifies his practical proficiency in English for professional communication and academic use.
